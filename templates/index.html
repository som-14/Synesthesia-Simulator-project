🎨 Synesthesia Experience Simulator

See the world through different senses

Synesthesia is a neurological condition where senses are "crossed." Some people see colors when they read letters. Others see shapes when they hear music. This simulator lets you experience how synesthetes perceive the world.

How It Works

1. Choose Your Input

Enter text, generate audio, or input a sequence of numbers/months.

2. Select a Profile

Use famous synesthete profiles (Nabokov, Messiaen) or create your own.

3. Generate Visualization

See your input transformed into colors, 3D maps, or animated spectrums.

4. Save & Export

Download your visualizations as images, CSV data, or text reports.
